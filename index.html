About the Film

Tom Holland returns as Peter Parker in Spider-Man: Brand New Day, bringing Marvel's iconic web-slinger back for an all-new adventure. In a world that completely forgot his secret identity after the events of No Way Home, Peter must navigate his isolated life while a dangerous, unseen new threat rises to tear the city apart. With no Avengers to call on and his closest friends unaware of who he truly is, Spider-Man faces his most personal battle yet.
