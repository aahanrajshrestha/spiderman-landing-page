THE STORYLINE

A CLEAN SLATE, A NEW DANGER

The World Forgot Peter Parker

Following the cataclysmic spell that fractured the multiverse, Peter Parker finds himself entirely alone. The world has forgotten his secret identity—but they have also completely forgotten him. No longer having access to Stark technology, the backing of the Avengers, or the comfort of his friends MJ and Ned, Peter is forced to start from scratch. Living in a cramped, run-down apartment in the heart of New York City, he attempts to balance his new job as a freelance photographer with his unending night shift as Spider-Man.

A New Threat Emerges

As Peter struggles with his isolation, a deep criminal vacuum opens up in the New York underworld. A mysterious and calculating new villain begins coordinating crime from the shadows, striking at the city's infrastructure and leaving a trail of high-tech destruction behind. Without his high-tech suits or tech support, Peter must rely purely on his raw spider-sense, his brilliant scientific mind, and his classic web-shooters to piece together a massive conspiracy.

The Ultimate Test of Responsibility

With the police hunting him down and the city turning against its vigilante protector, Spider-Man faces an impossible choice. How much is he willing to sacrifice to save a city that doesn't truly know him? Spider-Man: Brand New Day is a gritty, boots-on-the-ground return to the character's classic comic book roots, exploring what it truly means to wear the mask when you have nothing left to lose.
