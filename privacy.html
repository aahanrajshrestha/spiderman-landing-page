Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

When you visit the promotional site for Spider-Man: Brand New Day, we may collect technical information such as your IP address, browser type, and interaction metrics. If you sign up for fan updates, we collect the email address and regional data you explicitly provide.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to optimize site performance, deliver relevant geographical ticketing information, and securely send you promotional newsletters if you have opted in. Your data helps us ensure you get the most accurate premiere dates and nearby theater options.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share anonymized, aggregated data with trusted partners, including Sony Pictures Entertainment and local theatrical distributors, exclusively for the purpose of film marketing and ticket logistics. Data processing strictly adheres to regional privacy laws including GDPR and CCPA.

4. Cookies and Tracking

This site uses cookies to remember your preferences and enhance the cinematic loading experience. You may disable cookies in your browser settings; however, doing so may affect interactive elements like the trailer player or dynamic cast grid.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption protocols to protect any personal information submitted via our contact forms or newsletter sign-ups against unauthorized access or breaches.
